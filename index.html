Welcome

Letlotlo EMS is a 100% black owned company, it was founded by Ngwanagodimo Lucy Matabane in 2023. Letlotlo EMS is the sole provider os EMERGENCY MEDICAL SERVICES in Lebowakgomo, Limpopo and it’s incorporated cities. Exceptional services to our community with pride, Honesty and Integrity through efforts of compassion, teamwork and knowledge while maintaining the dignity of citizens we serve.

All Letlotlo EMS patients deserve high quality evidence-based care from the moment they call us for help, to the moment they are safely delivered to the hospital, we prioritise the safety of our patients. Patient safety will always remain a top priority at Letlotlo EMS. We provide 24/7 customer service.

Mission

Mission Statement: "At Letlotlo EMS, our mission is to save lives and serve the community with compassion, dedication, and professionalism. We strive to deliver prompt and reliable ambulance services, ensuring the well-being and safety of every individual we encounter."
